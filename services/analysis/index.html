SGE Economic & Policy Analysis

Our economic and policy team is ready to provide information on national and international commerce and policy.

what we do

            We present information on business laws and regulations of specific governments as they relate to your interests.

SGE provides technical support to people who are interested in getting involved with national and international trade. We present studies, information, and advice on bilateral and multilateral organizations, such as World Trade Organization, the African Union, regional organizations in Africa, United Nations, the International Monetary Fund, Overseas Private Investment Corporation, and the World Bank.

We have experts in quantitative business analysis, mathematical economics, economic and theory analysis, general operations research, cost-benefit analysis, welfare economics, theory of economic planning, econometric and statistical analysis, and international economics.

SGE has a rich experience in cost-benefit studies, economic modeling, trade policy research, and tariff and duty standards of several countries. We provide research on the economy, trade, and policy advice. We specialize in Africa, China, and the United States. We also provide policy and trade remedy recommendations to organizations and governments.
